CSTA Washington Quarterly Open Membership Meeting Mar 2025

Mar 05, 2025 6:30 PM - 8:00 PM Pacific Standard Time

Register
Interested in learning more about the new CSTA Washington and the work that we do? Please join us for our quarterly open meeting. In addition to our normal business of reviewing status and planning for future events, we will be discussing the work of the chapter development committee, updates from the CSTA Chapter Leadership Summit, and ideas for improving chapter finances. Please register to get an e-mail with a Zoom link for the meeting.
